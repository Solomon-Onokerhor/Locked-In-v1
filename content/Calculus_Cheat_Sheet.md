# Calculus 1: Master Cheat Sheet (Fundamentals) 📐⚡

The core concepts you need to "Lock In" for your first year of Calculus.

## 1. Limits: The Foundation
- **Definition**: What happens as $x$ gets infinitely close to $a$?
- **The Rule**: Always try direct substitution first. 
- **Indeterminate Forms ($0/0$ or $\infty/\infty$):** Use **L'Hôpital's Rule** (differentiate top and bottom).

## 2. Derivatives: The Rate of Change
- **Power Rule**: $\frac{d}{dx}[x^n] = nx^{n-1}$
- **Product Rule**: $(uv)' = u'v + uv'$
- **Chain Rule**: $\frac{d}{dx}[f(g(x))] = f'(g(x)) \cdot g'(x)$ (Essential for complex functions!)

## 3. Integrals: The Area Under the Curve
- **Fundamental Theorem of Calculus**: $\int_a^b f(x)dx = F(b) - F(a)$
- **U-Substitution**: The "Reverse Chain Rule." Perfect for when you see a function and its derivative in the same integral.

## 4. Common Derivatives to Memorize
- $\sin(x) \rightarrow \cos(x)$
- $\cos(x) \rightarrow -\sin(x)$
- $e^x \rightarrow e^x$
- $\ln(x) \rightarrow 1/x$
